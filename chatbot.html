Project Overview

The College FAQ Chatbot is an intelligent conversational system developed to assist students by answering frequently asked questions related to academics, admissions, examinations, placements, and campus facilities. The chatbot provides quick and accurate responses through a user-friendly interface, reducing the need for manual support and improving accessibility to important information.
